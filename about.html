ABOUT

Benefit Cardio Club is what you have been waiting for. We are ready to change your life. Our one-on-one & group personal training classes are equipped with the TRX & TRX Rip Trainer Training System that allows your body weight to burn calories and increase cardiovascular endurance. Safely perform exercises with the suspension training body weight system and build power, strength, flexibility & core, balance, mobility, and prevent injuries, all at the intensity you choose.

Group training sessions are becoming the fastest growing form of exercise. The ability to share a trainer as well as the 'experience' with others has become very popular. Benefit Cardio Club offers group personal training classes, five days a week. If you are training for an event, trying to lose a few pounds or just looking for a change in your exercise program, our group classes will raise your fitness level and help you reach your goals. The group classes are designed for people of all fitness levels who are looking for a new challenge or want to train above their potential. Our classes make it possible to work different muscle groups progressively during each week.
