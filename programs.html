PROGRAMS

1 on 1 Personal Training

Lets get you going! Whether you're just starting off, or want to change up your normal workout routine; private personal training will help reach your goals! 1 on 1 personal training includes a fitness plan customized and tailored to your needs as well as fitness assessment to track your accomplishments along the way.

Nutrition Coaching

Need someone to hold you accountable on your nutrition goals? How about learning how to interpret nutrition labels, learn nutrition terms, or how to properly select portion sizes to compliment your active lifestyle? Lets set up an appointment! Benefit Cardio Club can help you!

Semi Private Personal Training

Grab a partner lock in your own time and lets have some fun! We'll help reach those goals with your own fitness program tailored to your needs as well as your fitness assessment. 2-5 participants.

Group Personal Training

The ability to share a trainer as well as the experience with others has become very popular. Group Personal Training classes are designed for people of all fitness levels who are looking for a new challenge or want to train above their potential. Our classes make it possible to work different muscle groups progressively during each week. Classes incorporate circuit training, strength training, flexibility training, and much more. To help rev up that metabolism classes can include kettle bells, battling ropes, Bosu trainers, slosh pipes just to name a few!

In Home Personal Training

Tight on time? Train in the comfort of your own home! In Home personal training comes included with a fitness plan, and a fitness assessment.

Fitness Assessment

Each client will go through an assessment process to establish their needs and goals. Assessments include before and after body measurements with photos; as well as physical fitness test that will test strength, core strength, flexibility, speed, and cardiovascular endurance. Fitness assessments can be tailored to fit the individual.

Fitness Programming

Programs are personalized to benefit beginning exercisers to advanced clients as well as those interested in sports specific or post rehab training.

South Miami Boot Camp

Lets add some variety and go for a run! Looking to build some more endurance? Classes can include body weight exercises, H.I.I.T workouts, Tabata training, partner exercises and more!
