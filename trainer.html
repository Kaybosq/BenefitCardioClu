Angelo Bosques

Angelo Bosques is a certified personal trainer with over 10 years of experience in the health and fitness industry. He holds a Bachelor of Science in Dietetics and Nutrition from Florida International University.

His method is rooted in the belief that increased awareness of the critical relationship between physical conditioning and proper nutrition is the enlightened path to a more balanced body and focused mind. He has an extensive background in sports and athletics, and an in-depth experience with functional strength and body conditioning.

Angelo combines his knowledge of health and fitness with endless enthusiasm and a positive attitude, and he can bring energy to both the earliest morning and late night workouts! Working hand in hand with clients to achieve weight loss goals and to attain higher levels of physical fitness and mental endurance is what makes Angelo a sought after trainer and coach.

Angelo enjoys working closely alongside his clients to achieve their particular health and fitness goals. These goals include weight loss, improving general strength, tone and conditioning, targeted fitness training, and enhancing mental clarity and concentration.

A transformative physical journey begins with a detailed assessment. A tailored, personal fitness plan is designed for each client in order to attain their goals.
